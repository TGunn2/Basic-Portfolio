About me

Pengwei (Thomas ) Tian was an architect from Beijing who currently resides in San Francisco, CA. In 2017, he graduated with a Bachelor of Art in Architecture Design from University of Washington in Seattle.

In 2019, he graduated from Architecture graduate school of University of Michigan. Currently, he is attending the Berkeley Coding bootcamp, and wanting to be a front-end developer in the near future.

He wishes his design background in architecture can bring him success when he steps into another design and coding related field.
